Privacy Policy

Haaromfame: Amajjii 2025 · OromiaHub odeeffannoo kee akkamitti eegna?

1. Odeeffannoo Fuunuu

Galmaa'uu yoo godde odeeffannoo armaan gadii fuunna: maqaa, email, lakkoofsa bilbilaa, magaala. Hojii fayyadama irraa: posts, kuusaa, activity logs.

2. Odeeffannoon Maaliif Fayyadamama?

  • ✅ Account kee bulchuu fi security
  • ✅ Content sirrii si agarsiisuu (feed algorithm)
  • ✅ Kaffaltii mirkaneessuu
  • ✅ Platform fooyyessuu fi bugs sirreessuu
  • ❌ Gurgurtaa irratti hin dabarre
  • ❌ Namoota sadaffaaf hin kennamne (seera malee)

3. Odeeffannoo Eeguu

Data kee encryption AES-256 jalatti eegama. Servers Ethiopia / EU keessa jiru. Firebase security rules fayyadamna. Access log hunda 90 guyyaaf eegna.

4. Mirga Kee

  • 📋 Data kee ilaaluu – yeroo kamiyyuu request gochuu dandeessa
  • ✏️ Sirreessuu – profile settings irraa
  • 🗑️ Haqu – account delete gochuu dandeessa
  • 📦 Export – data kee download gochuu dandeessa

5. Cookies

Cookies xiqqoo fayyadamna: session (login eeguuf), preferences (theme, language). Analytics cookies hin fayyadamnu.

6. Daa'imman
